Instructions

If you are signing up with a partner, use the [ + ] button to add a new member and [ - ] button to remove a member. The max number of people allowed per team is Two people.

Please use your School emails. Some email domains may not be allowed. In the case where you are unable to register with a valid school email, please contact this email (info.ahscyber@gmail.com).

Your username and password will be sent to you after you complete the registration process.

There are cases where schools will actively block external emails from reaching you. If there is an issue with the registration process, please register here.
